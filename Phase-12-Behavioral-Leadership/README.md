## Story Bank Template:

For each story, prepare:

```
Title: [Short description]
Company/Context: [Where this happened]
Situation: [2-3 sentences of context]
Task: [What was your specific responsibility]
Action: [3-5 specific things YOU did]
Result: [Quantifiable outcome]
Learnings: [What you'd do differently]
LP Mapping: [Which leadership principles it demonstrates]
```

---

## Practice Tips:

1. **Record yourself**: Listen for filler words
2. **Time your stories**: 2-3 minutes each
3. **Practice with a partner**: Get feedback
4. **Prepare follow-ups**: Anticipate deep dives
5. **Be specific**: Names, numbers, timelines

## Common Interview Questions:

### Project & Technical

- "Tell me about a project you're proud of"
- "Describe a technical challenge you overcame"
- "Tell me about a time you made a wrong technical decision"
- "How do you prioritize technical debt?"

### Conflict & Collaboration

- "Tell me about a disagreement with a teammate"
- "How do you handle pushback on your ideas?"
- "Describe a time you had to convince others"
- "Tell me about working with a difficult stakeholder"

### Leadership & Growth

- "How have you mentored others?"
- "Tell me about a time you led without authority"
- "How do you stay current with technology?"
- "Describe a time you improved a process"

### Failure & Learning

- "Tell me about a failure"
- "Describe a time you missed a deadline"
- "What's the biggest mistake you've made?"
- "How do you handle criticism?"

---

## Red Flags to Avoid:

- Blaming others
- Taking credit for team work
- Vague, unquantified results
- Not taking ownership
- Speaking negatively about past employers
